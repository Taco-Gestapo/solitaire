Game Rules
  1. Game comprise of 3 sections, ie EXTRA, HOME & PLAYING AREA.
  2. EXTRA AREA has extra cards.
  3. HOME AREA must be filled with all 52 cards to win.
  4. Cards in HOME AREA must be placed in Accending order of card value & of same SUIT.
  5. Cards in PLAYING AREA must be placed in Decending order of card value & of alternate COLOUR.
  •  10 Points => Card moved inside HOME AREA
  • -12 Points => Card moved outside HOME AREA
  • -20 Points => Cheat Mode is used for invalid moves
  •  -2 Points => Card moved within PLAYING AREA
Features
  1. DOUBLE CLICK on a card, moves card inside HOME AREA if the move is valid.
  2. 'New Game' button creates a new game.
  3. 'Reset' button rearaange cards to initial state.
  4. 'Undo' button reverts a card move made.
  5. 'Cheat' button enables/disables cheat mode, ie Enables INVALID move.
